Putting in practice concepts learned about jQuery, Bootstrap, javaScript, Python and Django.

About this Project

The main goal of this project is to put in practice what I have learned so far about Bootstrap, javaScript, jQuery, Django, Python, Html and CSS. Building a "pet project" is an excellent way to test the skills learned and to "push the envelope" of our knowledge and time management skills.

It is really easy to study few weeks and feel confident about something new we have just learned. In the other hand, the so called "impostor syndrome" can be something that may cause real demage once it stops us from applying for jobs or trying new challenges because we never feel "ready". By building a project like this I feel confident enough to both put my programming skills and my English communication skills to proof in a job interview.
